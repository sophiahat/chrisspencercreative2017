Classic Wooden Boat Restoration

We'll bring your boat back to life...

From the simplest cosmetic makeovers to the most elaborate structural overhaul, we can turn back the clock for your yacht. Our attention to detail not only ensures aesthetic accuracy, but you can rest assured that your boat be more seaworthy than it ever was.

Most restorations involve repairing wood rot of some sort; which means planking, frames, chines, transoms, decks and bottoms are often replaced. While these repairs are more surgical in nature there is also a fair amount of aesthetic repair usually required as well, involving stripping, sanding, fairing and refinishing. In the end, we go beyond restoring the boat's strength and integrity, and give you a beautiful craft that is a pleasure to use.

Replacement Parts
