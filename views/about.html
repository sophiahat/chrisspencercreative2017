Background

With over 24 years in the business of making music and 10 years of running a recording studio, I have the ideal background and training in creation of audio soundscapes. I am the founder of Sophiahat Studios (www.sophiahatstudio.com) where we have created audio books, game audio, TV and radio spots, and short and full-length film work. I have been working as a freelance guitarist all of my adult life and continue to play gigs and teach on the side (www.chrisspencermusic.com). I take pride in both composing and licensing music, creating sound effects, and mixing and mastering the finished product; in essence, I'm the one stop solution for your audio needs.
